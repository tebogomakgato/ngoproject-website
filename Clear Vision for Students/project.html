Our Eye Care Projects

Here are some impactful projects that our NGO has undertaken. Click each project to learn more about it.

Eye Health Awareness Drive

We began our Eye Health Awareness Drive with an initial group of 50 students from local schools.

Learn more

Each student received a thorough eye examination, and those with identified issues were provided with corrective eyewear, including free spectacles tailored to their specific needs. By raising awareness about eye health and its importance in education, we aim to ensure that no child is held back by preventable vision impairments.

Vision Screening Program

In our Vision Screening Program, we expanded our efforts to reach an additional 100 students.

Learn more

This project focused on providing free vision screenings to detect any vision impairments. Each student underwent a comprehensive eye examination, and those needing corrective lenses received free spectacles to help them see better in their studies.

Eye Care Access Initiative

Our Eye Care Access Initiative aimed to extend our services to underserved areas, starting with a target of 150 students.

Learn more

We organized mobile eye clinics that visited local communities, offering free eye tests and consultations. Each student received personalized attention, and those diagnosed with vision issues were provided with free spectacles to address their specific needs.
